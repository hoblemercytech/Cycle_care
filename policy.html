Legal & Privacy

Your Data. Your Body. Your Control.

CycleCare is built to help women track their cycles with confidence. These policies explain how we handle your information, protect your privacy, and keep your account secure.

Privacy Policy

CycleCare respects your privacy. We collect only the information needed to create your account, save your cycle records, and provide personalized cycle and ovulation insights.

Information We May Collect

  • Your name and email address.
  • Your selected health goal.
  • Cycle logs such as period start date and period end date.
  • App usage data needed to improve the service.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your CycleCare account.
  • To calculate cycle length, period length, ovulation estimates, and fertile windows.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To protect your account and prevent misuse.

Health Disclaimer

CycleCare provides estimated cycle and fertility information based on the data you enter. It does not provide medical diagnosis and should not replace professional healthcare advice.

We do not sell your cycle or fertility data.

Terms of Service

By using CycleCare, you agree to use the app responsibly and understand that all predictions are estimates based on your cycle history.

Use of the App

  • You must provide accurate information for better predictions.
  • You are responsible for keeping your login details secure.
  • You should not use CycleCare for emergencies or urgent medical decisions.

Fertility & Pregnancy Disclaimer

CycleCare can help identify likely fertile days and ovulation windows, but it cannot guarantee pregnancy or prevent pregnancy. Ovulation can change due to stress, illness, hormones, medication, breastfeeding, irregular cycles, and other factors.

Account Access

We may restrict access to accounts that abuse the platform, attempt to compromise security, or violate these terms.

Cookie Policy

CycleCare may use cookies or browser storage to keep you logged in, remember basic preferences, and improve app performance.

What Cookies Help With

  • Keeping your login session active.
  • Remembering selected preferences.
  • Improving app speed and user experience.

Your Control

You can clear cookies or browser storage through your browser settings. However, doing so may log you out of your account.

Data Security

CycleCare uses secure authentication and database access rules to protect user information. Your profile and cycle data are connected to your own account.

How We Protect Your Data

  • Secure login through Supabase Authentication.
  • Row Level Security policies to limit data access.
  • User-specific records so users can only access their own data.
  • Admin access restrictions for management features.

Your Responsibility

Use a strong password, avoid sharing your login details, and always log out when using a shared device.

Your cycle information is sensitive. CycleCare is designed with privacy-first protection.
