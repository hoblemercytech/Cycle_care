❓ FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Everything you need to know before using CycleCare.

How accurate are ovulation predictions? +
CycleCare gives estimated ovulation and fertile window predictions based on your logged period history. The more cycles you track, the better the app can understand your pattern. Predictions are helpful guides, not medical guarantees.
Can CycleCare tell me the exact day I can get pregnant? +
No app can promise the exact day pregnancy will happen. CycleCare helps you identify your likely fertile window and ovulation period, which are the days pregnancy is more likely if you have unprotected sex.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. Your account is protected with secure authentication, and your cycle records are linked only to your personal account. CycleCare is designed to keep your fertility and period information private.
Can I track irregular cycles? +
Yes. You can log irregular periods, missed periods, and changing cycle lengths. For irregular cycles, predictions may be less accurate at first, but your average cycle length improves as you add more history.
Can CycleCare calculate my average cycle length? +
Yes. Once you log multiple periods, CycleCare calculates the number of days between each period start date and uses that to estimate your average cycle length.
Can CycleCare calculate my average period length? +
Yes. The app calculates your average period length by comparing your period start and end dates across your saved records.
Can CycleCare help with pregnancy planning? +
Yes. CycleCare highlights your estimated fertile days, ovulation day, and best time to try for pregnancy. It can support pregnancy planning, but it should not replace professional medical advice.
Can I use CycleCare to avoid pregnancy? +
CycleCare can show estimated fertile days, but it should not be used as your only birth control method. Ovulation can shift, so speak with a healthcare professional for reliable contraception advice.
Is CycleCare free? +
CycleCare can start with free core features such as period logging, cycle history, ovulation estimate, and fertile window tracking. Premium features can be added later.
